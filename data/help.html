Series Organizer v1.5
Series Organizer helps you keep track of the shows you watch.

How To Use:
Click the Add button on the main panel to begin adding a new entry. A new window will popup where you can enter the name of the show, the series and episode you are on, and a link to where you stream the episodes. Click the add button on this window when you are finished and your new entry will be added! Simply click the entry in the table and its link will open in a new tab.

Use the plus and minus buttons to quickly change the series and episode numbers in the table. The pencil icon allows you to edit the entry's values and the garbage can icon allows you to delete enteries.

Protip: You can also highlight a show name on a webpage then right-click and select "Add show to Series Organizer" to quickly begin adding an entry
